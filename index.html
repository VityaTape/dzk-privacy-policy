Privacy Policy for DZK Telegram Bot

Last updated: April 14, 2025

What data we collect

When you interact with the Bot, the following data may be stored:

  • Your Telegram user ID
  • Your Telegram username (nickname)
  • Your score (within the game)
  • Chat ID (where the game takes place)
  • Chat title and type (e.g. group, supergroup)

How we use this data

This data is used exclusively to power the functionality of the Bot, including:

  • Tracking player progress (scores)
  • Displaying player names and statistics
  • Managing game sessions within group chats

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. All data remains securely stored and is only used internally by the Bot.

Payment information

Monetization features (e.g. access to premium content) may be introduced in the future. If so, all payments will be handled via Telegram Payments, and we will not store or process any payment data ourselves.

Data retention and removal

We store your game data for as long as the Bot is active. You can request deletion of your data by contacting the bot admin via @V_Tape.

Changes

We may update this policy in the future. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
